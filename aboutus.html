Home Menu About Us

We are a small NY style pizza restaurant located in Johnson City, Tn. We offer indoor and outdoor seating, delivery, we have a drive through, offer beer on tap, pizza by the slice, and daily specials. We also offer a rewards program!


We began serving our delicious pizza when we first opened our doors in 1991.


We have won best local pizzeria in Johnson City several times in the past: 1996, 2007, 2008, 2015, and most recently in 2018.


Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide Johnson City and the surrounding local areas with another resource for our customers to enjoy our famous Luke's New York style pizza.
